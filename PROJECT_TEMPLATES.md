## ✨ PRO TIPS FOR PROJECT CARDS

1. **Keep it Scannable:** Use bold for key phrases
2. **Add Metrics:** Show real numbers (stars, downloads, users)
3. **Include Links:** Direct to live demo, docs, issues
4. **Use Emojis Strategically:** Max 1-2 per section
5. **Show, Don't Tell:** Include screenshots/GIFs
6. **Current & Honest:** Update status regularly
7. **Professional Tone:** Confident but not arrogant
8. **Call-to-Action:** End with clear next step
